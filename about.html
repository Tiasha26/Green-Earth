About Green Earth

Our mission is to create a sustainable future for generations to come

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Green Earth emerged from a shared vision of environmental activists and sustainability experts who recognized the urgent need for collective action against climate change.

What started as a small group of passionate individuals has grown into a global movement of environmental advocates, working together to protect our planet and promote sustainable practices.
